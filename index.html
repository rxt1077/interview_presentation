Scenario

You're a DBA for a medium sized school district (10K students). Recently you've won the "honor" of being audited by NJQSAC. In one of your buildings the students may be over 18 years old and have the option of signing themselves out. Unfortunately your Student Information System does not have a facility to keep track of this. As such, the attendance secretary keeps the data in a "system of his own." The auditors want you to run reports on how many students typically sign out each day and who is signing out. You reach out to the secretary who emails you "sign_outs.zip" which you will find in the /vagrant directory of this VM. Get the data into a sqlite3 database such that you can run useful reports on it.

Semester Project

Proposal

Students pick a field that interests them and describe how they would use a database to solve a problem in that field. The database should have a front end or scraping script for entering data and a reporting system for generating useful results. Students must consider what type of database would be best for solving their problem and describe what procedures / staff they would need to support it.

Semester Project

Example proposal

The specialty imports business requires a close working relationship with suppliers and customers. In this industry margins are extremely tight and business usually relies on a series of long-term, trust-based relationships. As such, many specialty import warehouses feel they do not need and sophisticated electronic inventory system. I propose to design a database for a specialty import business that they can use to keep track of their inventory. The database will have a web front-end for checking in deliveries and keeping track of shipments. It will also generate monthly reports that can be printed out. The entire operation can be set up and run in the cloud and will maintain automatic backups. The selling point of this system is that it can be administered remotely and does not require full-time IT staff. At the same time it is a custom system and can be adapted to specific customer needs.
